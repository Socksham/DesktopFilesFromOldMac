FOOD WHERE IT SHOULD BE

795 Million People Are Living Hungry But...

Just in the US, 150,000 TONS of food are wasted every day! A single restaurant produces up to 75,000 pounds of waste every year. How much of that is donated? A heartbreaking 1%.

















So, What Do We Do? Food Where It Should Be connects your restaurant or entertainment service to a nearby homeless shelter, or food dispensary. The left over food at your location will be used to feed the hungry. Help someone sleep on a full stomach today.

This program complies with and promotes the second global goal by globalgoals.org, ZERO HUNGER!
